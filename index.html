OneSquare for Foursquare

A simple yet efficient tool for Foursquare focused on only one function: check-in.

Why rebuild a tool?

Foursquare moved the check-in function to its Swarm app. Personally, I'm not quite satisfied with Swarm's check-in experience. When I want to confirm the place before check in, I have to take several steps to see the map and return to the check-in view. So I made this small tool to help me with the quick check-in.

  • It shows a proper scaled map preview of the check-in place. (You can use 3D Touch if your device supported)
  • The venue list can be sorted by distance according to your current location.

But why a footprint?

Personally, I use the check-in as a memo for the places I've been to. So as a one-person developed simple tool, a footprint is a good option for me :)
